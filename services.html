Servicing

Keep your vehicle running smoothly with our three servicing options. We use quality parts, deliver comprehensive care tailored to your needs, and handle everything at your location — no drop-off required.

Interim Service

Our Interim Service is designed for routine maintenance between major services. We change your engine oil and oil filter, check all fluid levels (coolant, brake fluid, washer fluid), and conduct a basic visual inspection of key components. This keeps your vehicle in good working order and is ideal if you drive moderate distances or want to maintain your car affordably throughout the year.

Basic Service

The Basic Service includes everything from the Interim Service, plus we replace your engine air filter and perform a more comprehensive inspection of your vehicle's systems. We examine wear patterns on brake pads and discs, check suspension components, inspect belts and hoses, and assess overall vehicle condition. This service strikes the right balance between thoroughness and cost, making it our most popular choice for regular vehicle maintenance.

Full Service

Our Full Service is the most comprehensive option we offer and includes everything from the Basic Service, plus replacement of your cabin filter and spark plugs (petrol engines) or fuel filter (diesel engines). We perform an extensive inspection using both visual assessment and specialist tools and testers to check brake condition, suspension wear, fluid condition, and overall vehicle health. This is the ideal service if your vehicle has higher mileage, you haven't serviced in a while, or you want complete peace of mind about your vehicle's condition.

  • • Quality parts supplied and fitted
  • • No garage drop-off required
