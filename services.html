Servicing

Three servicing tiers, all delivered at your location. Quality parts. Proper inspection. No drop-off needed.

Tier 01

Interim Service

Engine oil and filter change, fluid level checks, basic visual inspection. Ideal between major services for moderate-mileage drivers.

Tier 02

Basic Service

Everything in Interim, plus engine air filter replacement and a fuller inspection of brakes, suspension, belts and hoses. Our most popular tier.

Tier 03

Full Service

Everything in Basic, plus cabin filter and spark plugs (petrol) or fuel filter (diesel), with full diagnostic inspection. Complete peace of mind.

  • • Quality parts supplied and fitted
  • • No garage drop-off required
