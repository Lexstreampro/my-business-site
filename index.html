FAQs

Questions answered.

Are you as good as a proper garage?

Yes. We use the same diagnostic tools, manufacturer-spec parts, and proven procedures. The difference is we come to you and don't try to upsell you work you don't need.

Can you do serious diagnostics at my home?

Absolutely. We arrive fully equipped with professional diagnostic equipment. We can diagnose the vast majority of vehicle issues without needing a workshop. If something truly can't be done mobile, we'll tell you honestly.

Can you handle complex jobs, or just small repairs?

We handle servicing, brakes, batteries, diagnostics, repairs, and fleet support. Big jobs or small, we're equipped and qualified. Check our services page for the full scope.

How does transparent pricing work?

We provide a detailed estimate before any work starts. You approve it or decline. If extra issues are discovered during the job, we call and explain before proceeding. No surprises, no hidden charges.

What areas do you cover?

We serve South Wales — from Cardiff to Swansea, Caerphilly to the Valleys, and across the region. Check our coverage map to confirm your postcode.

Do you work with businesses and fleets?

Yes. We offer fleet support and repeat-customer arrangements. We can schedule regular maintenance for business vehicles at your premises. Get in touch for details.

How do I book? Can I see your calendar?

Use the booking form below to request a slot. You'll see real availability and can choose the date and time that works for you. It's transparent, no waiting for callbacks or vague "we'll let you know" responses.
